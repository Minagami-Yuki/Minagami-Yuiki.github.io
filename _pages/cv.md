Research Experience
======
* Research on Small Intelligent Inspection Vehicle for Railway Track Maintenance, 06.2020 – 06.2021
  * National College Students’ innovation and Entrepreneurship Training Program(Team Leader)
  * Ministry of Education of the People’s Republic of China
  * Aiming at the shortcomings of high cost and heavy labor in traditional railway track inspection and maintenance, a small railway inspection unmanned vehicle was designed and built.
  * The small target detection algorithm based on improved YOLOv5 is used to detect the bolt state and the track static foreign body.
  * A multi-target tracking algorithm based on DeepSort is used to detect the dynamic foreign body invasion of rail.
  * The autonomous positioning and mapping technology based on GMapping was used to facilitate the subsequent path planning.

* Research of Safety Monitoring Platform System for Railway Bridge Construction Support, 01.2021 – 01.2022
  * Science and Technology Innovation Ability Cultivation of College School Students(Team Leader)
  * Hebei Provincial Department of Science and Technology
  * Aiming at the problem that it is difficult to monitor the stress and deformation state of the support in the construction process of railway Bridges, a support safety early warning management platform system is established to realize remote management and control.
  * Based on STM32 chip, the data acquisition system is designed by using sensors.
  * Based on MQTT Internet of things protocol, a wireless data transmission system is designed.


    
* Design and Implementation of Wide-range Autonomous Positioning System for UGV Facing Wild Unstructured Environment, 09.2021 – 06.2022
  * Bachelor Thesis(STDU)
  * Aiming at the problem that the unmanned vehicle is difficult to locate itself in the complex field environment, the Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM) technology using lidar and vision fusion was studied, and a small unmanned vehicle platform was independently built for experimental verification.
  * A small unmanned vehicle experimental platform is built with Nvidia Jetson Nano as the main control.
  * Based on RTAB-Map, a positioning and mapping system for unmanned vehicles in a wide range of scenes is designed.
  * Based on the self-built experimental platform, different sensors are used to experiment and analyze the proposed algorithm in different field scenes.

 
* Research on High-precision 3D Reconstruction Technology of Visual SLAM based on Radiation Field, 12.2023-06.2025(expected)
